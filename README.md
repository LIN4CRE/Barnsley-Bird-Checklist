# 🐦 Barnsley Bird Checklist

A beautiful, simple, and "Mum-friendly" birdwatching app designed specifically for spotting birds around Barnsley and South Yorkshire.

## ✨ Features

- **Simple & Beautiful**: A clean, nature-inspired design that's easy on the eyes.
- **Celebrate the Spots**: A burst of confetti every time you find a bird!
- **Easy Sharing**: Send your progress to family and friends with a single tap.
- **Installable (PWA)**: Add it to your phone's home screen just like a real app.
- **Offline Ready**: Works even when you're out in the woods without signal.
- **Privacy First**: All your sightings and data stay on your phone.

---

## 📲 How to Get the App

The app is live at: **[https://lin4cre.github.io/Barnsley-Bird-Checklist/](https://lin4cre.github.io/Barnsley-Bird-Checklist/)**

### To install it on your phone:

**For iPhone (Safari):**
1. Open the link in **Safari**.
2. Tap the **Share** button (the square with an up arrow).
3. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**.

**For Android (Chrome):**
1. Open the link in **Chrome**.
2. Tap the **three dots (⋮)** in the top right corner.
3. Tap **"Install app"** or **"Add to Home screen"**.

---

## 🕊️ How to Use It

1. **Spot a Bird**: When you see a bird, find it in the list and tap the big **"⬜ Not Seen Yet"** button.
2. **Celebrate**: Watch the confetti! The button will turn green and say **"✅ Spotted!"**.
3. **Share Your Progress**: Tap the **"📤 Share My Progress"** button at the top to send a message to your family telling them how many birds you've found today.
4. **Search**: Use the search bar at the top if you're looking for a specific bird (like a "Robin" or "Blue Tit").
5. **Settings**: Tap the **Gear Icon (⚙️)** in the top right to change between Light and Dark mode, or to save a backup of your data.

## 🛠️ For Developers

This is a single-file Progressive Web App (PWA) built with Vanilla JS and CSS.

### Project Structure:
- `index.html`: The entire app (HTML, CSS, and JS).
- `manifest.json`: Configuration for phone installation.
- `sw.js`: Service Worker for offline support.
